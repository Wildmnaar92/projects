Colombian Cookie Glaze and Spread

Ingredients

Cream Cheese Spread

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 2 cups whipped topping
  • 2 cups of fruit

Glaze

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup organge juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Total Time: 15 mins
Makes: 12-18 cookies

This recipe is for a delicious sugar cookie glaze and frosting. When added to the cookies with fruit on top, you can transform ordinary sugar cookies into extraordinary sugar cookies. This recipe is great for creating a simple dessert and is fun for the whole family. A separate recipe is needed for the sugar cookies.

Instructions

    Cream Cheese Spread

  1. Bake cookies and cool
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and powdered sugar until smooth
  3. Fold in whipped topping, set aside

    4. Glaze

  4. In a small saucepan, combine all glaze ingredients, bring to boil and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly until thickened
  5. Pour into small bowl and place in fridge until cooled but not set

    6. Assemble Sugar Cookie Fruit Pizzas

  6. Spread each cookie with cream cheese spread, top with fruit and drizzle with glaze
  7. Refrigerate for 1 hour or until chilled
